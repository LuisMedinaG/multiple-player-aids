Setup
1Place the board. Set the Emperor's Dial to 7 (5–6 for shorter game).
2Shuffle each trade good deck separately; place face-down in their spaces.
3Populate the Imperial Market: fill all 5 slots from the corresponding decks.
4Place Region tiles face-up in each region. Shuffle the remaining tiles face-down beneath them.
5Each player takes a player board, 1 ship, and places their merchant on the Start port.
6Deal 3 cards to each player. Reveal Journey cards equal to player count.
7Place Divine Favour tokens on shrines per rulebook. Give each player 3 Koku .
8Randomly determine first player; give them the start player marker.
Your Turn
1Play a card from hand — take the printed action.
Each card grants: Build, Sail, Deliver, or a special action.
2Spend Divine Favour to copy any card already played this turn.
3Refill hand to 3 cards from your personal deck (or shuffle discard).
4Pass the active-player token clockwise.
The Emperor's Dial advances 1 step at the end of each full round.
Actions
Build a Building
1Pay the cost shown on the Region tile in goods or Koku .
2Place a building token (Manor , Port , or Shrine ) in the region.
3Take the immediate bonus printed on the tile.
Manors score VP. Ports unlock extra cargo. Shrines grant Divine Favour .
Sail
1Move your ship up to your speed spaces along the river.
2Collect goods or Koku from each port you pass through or stop at.
3You may upgrade your ship by paying the cost — increases speed & cargo.
Cannot carry more goods than your cargo limit. Discard excess before moving.
Deliver
1Discard the goods shown on an Imperial Market card at your current port.
2Take the card; gain its Influence and any printed bonus.
3Refill the market slot immediately from the matching deck.
Abilities marked activate after delivery resolves.
Complete a Journey
1Meet the goods requirement shown on the Journey card.
2Discard the goods; claim the card and its VP + bonus.
3Reveal a new Journey card from the deck.
Moon markers on journey cards modify timing of rewards.
Emperor's Visit
When the dial reaches 0, scoring is triggered.

Each player with fewer buildings than the leader loses Influence equal to the difference.

The dial resets; game ends after the final visit.
